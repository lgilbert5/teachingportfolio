About Me

My name is Liam Gilbert. I am currently a third-year students at the Australian Catholic University (ACU).

I am minoring in Food Technology/ Food Science and Computer Science at the University too, while also having a wide range of skills in other areas such as multimedia, textiles, and agriculture.
